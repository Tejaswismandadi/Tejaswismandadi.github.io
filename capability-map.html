For Recruiters & Hiring Managers

If your job posting reads like this, here's where the proof is.

Supplier risk job descriptions tend to use the same handful of phrases — identify, monitor, escalate, govern, report. Below, each one is matched to the specific case study where it actually happened, in plain terms, including the gaps.

Risk Identification & Prioritization
"Identify supplier risk across capacity, logistics, single-source dependency, and Tier2/TierN exposure."
Built the P1/P2/P3 tiering logic at Nexteer, scoring single-source dependency, revenue impact, and lead time. Extended into AGNA's TTR/TTS criticality testing and structured sub-tier disclosure process.
Hidden Concentration Risk
"Identify critical Tier 2/Tier N dependencies."
Independently identified the diamond problem during the Nexteer engagement, then formalized detection logic for it in AGNA, demonstrated on the NovaDrive Motors case study.
Monitoring & Early Warning
"Monitor external data sources and internal metrics for early warning signals."
Designed the Power BI dashboard at Nexteer integrating Everstream data with quality and tier signals, plus a proxy intake path for suppliers with no public financial disclosure. Identified that Nexteer's own internal engineering signals were faster and more reliable than external disclosure — the same logic built into AGNA's early-signal library.
Escalation & Governance
"Escalate critical issues to leadership; adhere to risk governance frameworks."
Designed a 24-hour escalation protocol at Nexteer. Formalized into a full governance structure in AGNA — named risk ownership, pre-agreed thresholds, and pre-authorized emergency budget, set before any risk is scored.
Mitigation Planning
"Track supply continuity action plans — root cause, recovery timelines, next steps."
Built TTR/TTS methodology linking recovery timelines directly to buffer and safety stock sizing at Nexteer. AGNA operationalizes this further — every critical risk gets a named owner, deadline, cost, and measurable target state.
Executive Reporting
"Weekly risk reports and executive summaries for leadership visibility."
Presented the REI framework to Nexteer leadership in a structured executive format. AGNA formalizes this into a one-page board summary anchored in ROI figures.
Risk Dashboards & Tools
"Maintain and improve risk dashboards (Power BI or similar)."
Built a Power BI dashboard for Kearney's CBAM tool across 800+ suppliers in 7 countries, reducing manual effort ~80%. Built a second Power BI dashboard at Nexteer. AGNA designs a live dashboard around six KRI categories.
Multi-Tier Transparency & Digital Tools
"Support development of digital tools for supplier risk management, including Tier N mapping."
AGNA itself is the proof point — a working platform designed and built end-to-end to demonstrate Tier N mapping, diamond node detection, and regulatory risk propagation as digital tools, not a framework on paper.
Regulatory & Compliance
"Maintain compliance with customer, regulatory, and internal audit requirements."
Built the Kearney CBAM compliance tool, tracking documentation across 800+ suppliers against evolving EU carbon border requirements — the strongest, most directly applicable proof point for regulatory compliance work.
Sourcing & Tariff Strategy
"Segment supply base by risk; build tariff-aware sourcing strategy."
Led the sourcing workstream at the ISM Global Case Competition — dual-sourcing for life-critical items, near-shoring for tariff-exposed consumables — on the team that won 1st place globally.
Honest Gaps

Where I'd be building, not claiming.

Two things worth stating plainly rather than letting a job description imply otherwise: I haven't yet chaired standing daily/weekly risk review meetings as the primary owner, and I haven't run supplier onboarding due diligence as an ongoing operational responsibility. Both are natural next steps from the framework design work above — not things I'd claim to have already done.
